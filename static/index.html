About

ASTechLab is a technology studio led by Andrey Suhodeev, with a dedicated IoT & ICS Division focused on connected devices and industrial control systems. We design firmware and gateways on ESP32, build Wi-Fi and Bluetooth / BLE connectivity layers, and integrate them with reliable backends, dashboards, and tooling — with a soft spot for Rust.
