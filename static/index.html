About

ASTechLab is a technology studio led by Andrei Suhodeev, with a dedicated IoT & ICS Division focused on connecting physical systems with digital solutions. We design firmware and gateways on ESP32 and STM32, build Wi-Fi and Bluetooth / BLE connectivity layers, and tie sensors, actuators and machines into reliable backends and dashboards — with a soft spot for Rust.

Where it adds real value, we integrate neural-network solutions at the edge and in the cloud — from on-device anomaly detection on microcontrollers to vision and signal models running alongside industrial control loops, turning raw Internet of Things data into actionable decisions.
